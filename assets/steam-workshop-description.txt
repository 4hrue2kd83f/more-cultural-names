This is a mod for various strategy games, that adds new place names based on their culture/faction.

The names are meant to provide an immersive and accurate experience for the game's setting and time period.

Supported games:
 - [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1175098675]Crusader Kings 2[/url] with [url=https://ck2.paradoxwikis.com/Historical_Immersion_Project]Historical Immersion Project[/url]
 - [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1745899430]Crusader Kings 2[/url] with [url=https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=1429073254]Elder Kings[/url]
 - [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2217534250]Crusader Kings 3[/url]
 - [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2219177532]Imperator: Rome[/url]

The common database currently has over [b]45 thousand[/b] names for over [b]400[/b] languages, settings and time periods.

[img=https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hmlendea/more-cultural-names/master/assets/banner_compatibility.png][/img]

The mods are [b]OS-agnostic[/b], which means that they will work on all of the operating systems supported by the game.

It is also actively tested (and developed) on [b]Linux[/b], where it is guaranteed to work with either the native version of the game or through Steam Play.

It is also [b]save-game compatible[/b], and can be added and removed at will without breaking anything.
